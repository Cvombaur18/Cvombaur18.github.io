A little about me:

I recently graduated from Devmountain specializing in IOS software development. Since graduationg I have learned data and data analytics, so I decided to learn all i could about spreadsheets, pivot tables, charts, PostgreSQL, tableau, and some R coding language. Before changing career paths into tech, I worked for Kroger starting out as a bagger and working my way up to produce/floral coordinator.

As a Produce/Floral coordinator for Kroger I had to pull data from their database to find how much of a product was sold or not sold in a given time period to determine trends. I would then have to move this data over to a spreadsheet to help with proper inventory control, provide goals, and predict future trends. I would have to communicate this through email, meetings, and visiting stores to follow up on how well they are using the data. I would send out daily, weekly, and monthly reports on these goals to be sure we were on track with our sales goals.
